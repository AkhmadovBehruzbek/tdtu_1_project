About

Funded by the European Commission through the KA2 – Capacity Building for Higher Education action of the Erasmus+ programme, MechaUz will have a duration of 3 years and be implemented between 15th November 2019 and 14th November 2022.

Funded by the European Commission through the KA2 – Capacity Building for Higher Education action of the Erasmus+ programme, MechaUz will have a duration of 3 years and be implemented between 15th November 2019 and 14th November 2022. The main aim of the project is to develop Bachelor’s degree program in the field of Mechatronicsand Robotics to improve the mechatronics and robotics specialist’s knowledge in Automation sphere, to support the capacity building of higher education institutions and to make contribution for the sustainability of Mechatronics and Robotics.
